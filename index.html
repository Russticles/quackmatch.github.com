QuackMatch is the ultimate and original platform for assisting birds of all kinds to find their ideal partner.

We don't just cater for ducks, but all feathered friends from turkeys to chickens and quails to ibises. Whatever you're into, we'll find an echo for your quack.

Whether your looking for someone to settle down with during hibernation, a friend with migration benefits or just a casual chirp - we've got the songbird for you!
